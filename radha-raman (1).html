Shri Radha Raman
Mandir, Vrindavan

Shri Radha Raman Mandir, Vrindavan ke sabse praacheen aur paavitra mandiro mein se ek hai. Yahan Shri Gopal Bhatt Goswami ji ne san 1542 mein Bhagwan ko prakat kiya tha.

Radha Raman ji Shaligram Shila se swayam prakat hue hain — isliye ye saakshat Bhagwan hain. Har roz unka darshan ek divya anubhav hai jo mann ko shanti deta hai. 🙏
